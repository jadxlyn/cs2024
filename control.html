Nintendo Switch Parental Controls

Empower your family with tools to manage game time and content

Play Safely, Play Smart

Nintendo Switch Lite offers robust parental control features that put you in charge of your family's gaming experience. Easily manage what your children play, when they play, and how they interact online—all from your smartphone or the console itself.

Purchase Restrictions

Set limits on Nintendo eShop spending and require password approval for purchases.

Playtime Limits

Set daily play time limits and schedule automatic shutdown times.

Content Filtering

Restrict games based on age ratings and control access to age-restricted content.

Mobile App Control

Manage all parental controls remotely via the free Nintendo Switch Parental Controls app.
