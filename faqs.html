Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your Nintendo Switch Lite

How do I set up my Nintendo Switch Lite for the first time?

Follow these steps:

  1. Press the POWER Button to turn the system on
  2. Select the language you want to use
  3. Accept the End User License Agreement
  4. Connect to your Wi-Fi network
  5. Set your time zone
  6. Create or link a Nintendo Account
  7. Set up parental controls (optional)

For a more detailed guide, please see our Technical Support.

How long does the Nintendo Switch Lite battery last?

The Nintendo Switch Lite can last approximately 3 to 7 hours on a single charge, depending on the game you're playing and your brightness settings. Games with more intensive graphics will drain the battery faster.

To maximize your battery life:

  • Lower the screen brightness
  • Turn on airplane mode when not using wireless communications
  • Enable auto-sleep mode in system settings
What games are compatible with Nintendo Switch Lite?

Nintendo Switch Lite is compatible with all Nintendo Switch games that support handheld mode, indicated behind the game packaging and the Nintendo eShop.

Games that require detached Joy-Con controllers (like 1-2-Switch or Nintendo Labo) would require separate Joy-Con controllers to be purchased and connected to your Switch Lite.

Can I connect my Nintendo Switch Lite to a TV?

No, the Nintendo Switch Lite cannot be connected to a TV. It's designed exclusively for handheld play and does not include the necessary hardware to output video to an external display.

Does the smaller screen make it harder to read things?

The Nintendo Switch Lite is set up so that text doesn't blur, but if you feel that it is too small to read, the screen zoom function may assist you.

How do I add friends on Nintendo Switch Lite?

To add friends on your Nintendo Switch Lite:

  1. From the HOME Menu, select your user profile icon
  2. Select "Add Friend" from the left sidebar
  3. Choose one of the following methods:
    • Search with Friend Code
    • Search for Local Users
    • Search for Users You've Played With
    • Search for Users You've Added as Friends on Smart Devices
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to send a friend request

Note: You'll need to be connected to the internet and have a Nintendo Switch Online membership for some friend features.

How do I purchase and download games on my Nintendo Switch Lite?

Purchasing and downloading games on your Nintendo Switch Lite is easy:

  1. Make sure your console is connected to the internet
  2. From the HOME Menu, select the Nintendo eShop icon
  3. Select your user account
  4. Browse games or use the search function to find specific titles
  5. Select a game to view its details page
  6. Select "Proceed to Purchase" and follow the payment instructions
  7. Once purchased, the game will begin downloading automatically

You can also redeem download codes by selecting "Redeem Code" in the Nintendo eShop menu.

What is Nintendo Switch Online and do I need it?

Nintendo Switch Online is a paid subscription service that provides the following benefits:

  • Play games online with friends and players worldwide
  • Access to a growing library of NES and SNES classic games
  • Cloud backup for your save data (for supported games)
  • Special offers and promotions exclusive to members
  • Use of the Nintendo Switch Online smartphone app

You'll need Nintendo Switch Online if you want to play most games online, but it's not required for single-player experiences or local multiplayer. Some free-to-play games like Fortnite don't require a subscription to play online.

Can you connect a Joy-Con?

Handheld Mode-compatible games can be played with the Nintendo Switch Lite console alone, without a Joy-Con.

Joy-Con controllers (sold separately) can be used with the Nintendo Switch Lite via wireless connection.

For a more detailed guide, please see our Technical Support.

Our support team is ready to assist you with any other questions you might have about your Nintendo Switch Lite.
