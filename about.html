Our Journey

The Beginning

StarLITE didn't start with a warehouse full of consoles or a big corporate team. It started with a small group of gamers, dreamers, and lifelong Nintendo fans who simply wanted to share the joy of gaming with more people.

Growing Up

We grew up with Mario, raced our friends in Mario Kart, and got lost in the world of Zelda. But what we loved most was how gaming brought people together—whether it was siblings bonding over a boss battle or friends competing during a road trip.

Sharing Our Love

That's where the idea for StarLITE was born: A place where anyone, whether you're a seasoned gamer or picking up a console for the first time, can get the Nintendo Switch Lite easily, affordably, and confidently.
